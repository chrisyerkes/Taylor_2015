Our Philosophy

Since 1992, Taylor has helped shape brand destinies through deep strategic understanding and an innovative mindset. Our work is never by the numbers. And we don’t simply apply our aesthetic to our clients’ projects. Business performance, marketing flexibility, real time analytics, 1-to-1 customer connections, your sales and marketing goals—these are the things that drive our decisions and spark our creativity.

Our Strategy

At Taylor, our culture breeds ideas, creativity and innovation. Our staff combines the wisdom of senior strategists and senior art directors with the vitality of younger designers and programmers. All players move easily within the flow of each job—bouncing off each other, welcoming great ideas, seeking opinions and, with the help of our two business managers, making sure we stay on budget and on deadline.

Hey, listen: opportunity’s knocking

Our growth is limited to the high-level talent that we attract. So we’re always on the lookout for someone special. Is that you? careers@taylordesign.com.

+– Intern Position

The ideal candidate is passionate about the art and detail of design, gets brand strategy and storytelling, and thrives in a collaborative environment. Must love ice cream.

Must also have:

  • Relevant work experience or be a 3rd year graphic design student.
  • Strong knowledge of the Adobe suite, especially Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign. AfterEffects is a plus.
  • Impressive portfolio of design work across identity, packaging, digital, retail/environments, and advertising.
  • Strong strategic instincts and sense of curiosity.
  • Desire to work on a wide range of projects.
  • Willingness to dive in to help on both administratively boring and creatively exciting stuff.
  • Passions outside work that make lunch conversations more interesting.

If this sounds like you, please send a cover letter, résumé, and a link to digital samples of your work to careers@taylordesign.com. No printed portfolios until the interview, please.
