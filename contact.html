Think you may be a good fit here?

Taylor Design is growing and we’re always looking for talented and inquisitive folks to join our team. If you’re a designer or developer, drop us a line at careers@taylordesign.com.

+– Intern Position

The ideal candidate is passionate about the art and detail of design, gets brand strategy and storytelling, and thrives in a collaborative environment. Must love ice cream.

Must also have:

  • Relevant work experience or be a 3rd year graphic design student.
  • Strong knowledge of the Adobe suite, especially Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign. AfterEffects is a plus.
  • Impressive portfolio of design work across identity, packaging, digital, retail/environments, and advertising.
  • Strong strategic instincts and sense of curiosity.
  • Desire to work on a wide range of projects.
  • Willingness to dive in to help on both administratively boring and creatively exciting stuff.
  • Passions outside work that make lunch conversations more interesting.

If this sounds like you, please send a cover letter, résumé, and a link to digital samples of your work to careers@taylordesign.com. No printed portfolios until the interview, please.
